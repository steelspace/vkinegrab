## Notes

- **CineStar Anděl — Gold Class temporarily closed**: The Gold Class auditorium at CineStar Anděl is temporarily closed for reconstruction (as of early 2026).
- **Cinema City Letňany** has the largest single auditorium in Prague (476 seats, panoramic screen).
- **Cinema City Flora** features the only IMAX screen in Prague.
- **Kino Ponrepo** is operated by the National Film Archive (NFA) and focuses on archive and retrospective screenings; box office opens only on screening days.
- **Kino Lucerna** (est. 1909) is the oldest permanent cinema in Bohemia.
- **Kino Balt** opened in 2023 in Praha 3 (Žižkov) as a new small art-house venue inside the Prostor Balt cultural space.
- **Kino Kavalírka** reopened in Praha 5 (Smíchov) after renovation; focuses on art-house and themed evenings with drinks.
- **Přítomnost Boutique Cinema** is Prague's first boutique cinema (Praha 3 – Žižkov), with a bar inside the auditorium; cashless payments only.
- **Edison Filmhub** (Praha 1) is a newer art-house + lecture venue combining film screenings with discussions.
- The database tracks 32 venues total (scraped from CSFD); this includes occasional screening venues (theatres, libraries, bar-cinemas) alongside dedicated cinemas.
